# Trinity 5

- I am in awe of the staggering violence and lust for power that human beings are capable of.
- I am equally in all of the staggering generosity of spirit that ordinary human beings show on a regular basis 
- I am in all of the way in which the frequencies of sound form harmonies and dissonances, and that those same harmonies can sing a love song
