# Trinity 4

But in all honesty there is another way that God -- the Trinity -- has been used through the centuries. It has been used to distinguish between **us** and **them**. There is the right way to think about God and there is the wrong way to understand God. If you’re not with us you’re against us. Christians against Jews. Christians against the Roman Empire.

Christians against the Muslims. All of them “heathen”. The Trinity has been used for violent purposes all too often in our history.
